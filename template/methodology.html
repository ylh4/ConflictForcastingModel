Class imbalance

In reality, conflict occurs in a rare like situation resulting in a significant class imbalance in the output data between conflict and non-conflict events. EasyEnsemble Classifier

Undersampling is among the popular methods of handling class-imbalance. This method entails taking a subset of the major class to train the classifier. However, this method has a main deficiency as it ignores portions of the dataset in an attempt to balance the output records.

Xu-Ying, Jianxin, and Zhi-Hua (2080), proposed EasyEnsemble classifier to overcome the above deficiency of under sampling. The idea of EasyEnsemble forecast samples several subsets from the majority class and combines for a final decision. These independent amples ultimately take into account the different aspects of the entire dataset.
